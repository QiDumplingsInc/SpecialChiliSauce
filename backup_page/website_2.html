Our Story

我们的故事

Kay, known fondly as "Kay Jie" among her friends, was born in Xi'an, China. A passionate food lover and spice enthusiast, she began her culinary journey in Shenzhen, where she mastered the art of balancing flavors and heat. Bringing together the essence of both Northern and Southern Chinese chili traditions, Kay created her own signature chili sauce recipe — one that quickly gained admiration from friends and diners alike.


In 2013, Kay immigrated to North America, bringing her love of bold flavors across the ocean. Her homemade chili sauce soon became a bridge of friendship — a taste of home that connected people through shared meals and stories.


During her time running a restaurant in Virginia, Kay's chili sauce became a sensation. It was so popular that customers often asked for jars to take home, and even years after Kay sold her restaurant during the pandemic, people continued to reach out, hoping to get another taste.


To honor that passion and bring authentic heat to those who love spice as much as she does, Kay founded her own food company. Her chili sauce became the first product — and the first premium chili sauce cooked entirely with avocado oil on the market.


Today, Kay Chili Sauce carries the warmth of friendship, the richness of tradition, and the joy of real flavor — crafted for those who simply can't live without spice.

2013年，Kay移民到北美，将她对浓郁风味的热爱带到了大洋彼岸。她的自制辣椒酱很快成为了友谊的桥梁——一种通过共享的餐食和故事连接人们的家的味道。


在弗吉尼亚州经营餐厅期间，Kay的辣椒酱引起了轰动。它如此受欢迎，以至于顾客经常要求带罐子回家。即使在疫情期间Kay卖掉餐厅多年后，人们仍然不断联系，希望再次品尝到那个味道。


为了向这份热情致敬，并为像她一样热爱辣味的人们带来正宗的辣味，Kay创立了自己的食品公司。她的辣椒酱成为了第一款产品——也是市场上第一款完全使用鳄梨油烹制的高级辣椒酱。


今天，Kay辣椒酱承载着友谊的温暖、传统的丰富和真实风味的快乐——专为那些离不开辣味的人们精心制作。
