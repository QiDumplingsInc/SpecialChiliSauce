Our Story

After years of living abroad, Kay longed for the fiery spoonful of chili sauce that once accompanied every meal at home. She began making her own, bottling not just the flavor, but the memories.


Kay's chili sauce is more than a condiment — it's a connection to home, rich with heat and heart. Each bottle is handcrafted using her family's traditional recipe, made with carefully sourced, natural ingredients using authentic Chinese oil chili technique.
